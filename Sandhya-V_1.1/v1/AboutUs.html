About Us

Welcome to Hogging Diaries!

This is a place where we share our passion with the world - delicious healthy recipes. Everything you see here is made from scratch with non-processed natural foods. Most of our recipes are Indian, dairy, gluten, and spices and they contain a list of all of the goodness that you will treat your body to by preparing and eating them.

The website is targeted towards amateur cooks and foodies who want to try out new dishes, be it plain simple food for college kids or for people who want to try something new. Our main idea is to have refreshing recipes so that users keep on coming for something new. One way of doing this is by showing ‘Recipe of the day’.
